No, this Application does not collect precise real-time location information of your mobile device.

The Service Provider will retain user-provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. If you would like the Service Provider to delete user-provided data, please contact us at contact@alakhiar.site and we will respond within a reasonable time.
